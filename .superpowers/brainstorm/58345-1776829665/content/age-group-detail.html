B: Batch-First with "Log Visit"

Single prominent "Log Visit" button at top. Tapping it opens a sheet with all remaining vaccines as checkboxes — check the ones given, pick the date, add notes, save. No per-vaccine log buttons cluttering the list. Simulates the real experience: a doctor visit gives multiple vaccines at once.
